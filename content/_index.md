Hi, I'm **Guanyi Yang**, a master's student in Applied Statistics at the University of Michigan.

My work focuses on **statistical modeling, semiparametric inference, diagnostics, and high-dimensional data analysis**, with applications to cognitive aging, global health, and behavioral science. I develop reproducible pipelines in R and Python for simulation, estimation, and validation of statistical methods in complex empirical settings.

### **Research Interests**
- Statistical modeling for complex population data  
- Semiparametric & nonparametric methods  
- High-dimensional inference (Sparse PCA, clustering, dimension reduction)  
- Multinomial & ordinal regression modeling  
- Missing data mechanisms; sensitivity analyses  
- Diagnostics, goodness-of-fit, overlap assessment  
- Bayesian modeling & hierarchical structures
